The Data Analytics project exploring the ball by ball CSV data of all T20 cricket matches from 2015 to 2024.

This project involved Data Cleaning, Data Pre-processing, and Visualization in Python. The project uses various libraries such as Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. Various CSV files are generated contributing useful data such as:

  • Statistics of every ground that are hosting 2024 T20 World Cup.
  • Statistics of every team participating in T20 World Cup.
  • Performance of every team across each phase (powerplay, middle overs, and death).
