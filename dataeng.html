F1 DataBase Project

The project involved working with a comprehensive dataset containing detailed information on Formula 1 races, drivers, constructors, qualifying sessions, circuits, lap times, pit stops, and championships spanning from 1950 to the 2023 season. The dataset was normalized according to the principles of Third Normal Form (3NF) and Boyce-Codd Normal Form (BCNF). Utilizing this normalized data, a website was developed for efficient data retrieval. Optimized indexing strategies were applied to enable the website to manage complex queries swiftly and effectively.
